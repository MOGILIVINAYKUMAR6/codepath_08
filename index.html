About the Issue

Informal workers often face severe challenges like financial instability, lack of healthcare, and mental health issues. Our goal is to raise awareness and offer actionable solutions to improve their well-being.

Common Issues

Financial Instability

Many informal workers live paycheck to paycheck, facing chronic stress.

Lack of Support

With limited access to mental health services, workers often feel isolated.

Workplace Hazards

Exposure to unsafe environments increases anxiety and other health risks.

Potential Solutions

  • Accessible mental health care services tailored for informal workers.
  • Financial aid programs and social safety nets.
  • Workplace safety regulations and stress management programs.
